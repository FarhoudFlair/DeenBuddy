1. Introduction

Welcome to DeenBuddy ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

DeenBuddy is designed to be a private, personal companion for your spiritual journey. We believe your faith is between you and Allah (SWT), and your data should reflect that privacy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Prayer tracking data: Your prayer completion records and streaks
  • Tasbih counts: Your dhikr counter progress
  • App preferences: Calculation method, madhab, notification settings
  • Your name (optional): Used only for personalized greetings within the app

2.2 Location Data

We request location access to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • Determine Qibla direction

Your location data is processed on-device or sent to our prayer time API only to fetch times. We do not store your location history on our servers.

2.3 Analytics (Anonymous)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, such as:

  • Feature usage patterns (which screens are visited)
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility)

This data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you personally.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal data we collect to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to your feedback or support requests

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Ever.

We may share anonymous, aggregated analytics data with:

  • Firebase (Google): For crash reporting and anonymous analytics
  • Prayer Time APIs: Your approximate location to retrieve prayer times

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not store sensitive data on external servers

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app in Settings
  • Delete: Clear all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke location permissions at any time

8. Children's Privacy

DeenBuddy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
